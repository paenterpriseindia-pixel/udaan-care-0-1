## Tips

- **Format:** JPG for all photos. PNG only for logos (transparency needed).
- **Compression:** Use [squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app) — target under 300KB per image.
- **Portrait photos:** Shoot on a clean, neutral background. Good lighting is essential.
- **Clinic photos:** Natural lighting, no clutter. Capture the therapy room, waiting area, and entrance.
- **Hero background:** Can be a wide photo of the clinic exterior, therapy in session, or a professional abstract medical background.
